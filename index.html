Set up your tournament in four steps

Designed for a volunteer doing this the night before with no tech expertise needed.

1

Enter your constraints

Teams, pitches, match length, start time, hard end time. Takes about 3 minutes.

2

Pick your format

The AI suggests 2–3 options in plain English — pros, cons, estimated finish time. You choose, Féile generates the draw.

3

Share the registration link

Drop one link in your WhatsApp group. Managers open it and see everything they need — no sign-up, no app.

4

Run the day

Enter scores on your phone. Standings update live for everyone. Send the public link and let the day run itself.

Everything you need, nothing you don't

From the night before right through to the final whistle.

AI that recommends the right format for your day

Just fill in your constraints and the AI engine returns 2–3 format options with plain-English trade-offs — no sports management experience required. It feasibility-checks each option against your actual numbers so nothing gets recommended that can't fit.

  • Green / amber / red feasibility at a glance
  • Estimated finish time per option
  • Round Robin, Groups + Knockout, Blitz and more

AI Format Suggestion

Groups of 4 + Cup & Shield Recommended

4–6 games per team · Finishes ~14:45

Every team plays at least 4 games. Clear progression path. Works perfectly with 3 pitches and a 15:00 finish.

Full Round Robin Tight

6 games per team · Finishes ~15:20 (over)

More games but risks running over. Consider if finals aren't required.

Full schedule generated in seconds

Féile builds a complete fixture list — pitch allocation, kick-off times, rest intervals. It flags conflicts before they cause problems on the day, then lets you drag-and-drop to adjust anything you want.

  • Conflict warnings for back-to-back fixtures
  • Drag-and-drop to swap fixtures or teams
  • Export as PDF by pitch or by team
