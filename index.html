Products

Two products, grounded in real club needs.

Sideline Community already has two focused products that show the kind of problems we solve for grassroots sport.

Sideline

Run the club from the sideline.

A mobile-first club management app focused on communication, events, RSVPs, payments, and helping coaches, parents, and admins work from one place.

  • Reduce app switching between chat, events, fees, and family schedules.
  • Built for coaches, parents, treasurers, and club admins.
  • Designed to be simple on a phone, not heavy on admin.
Sideline Tourneys

Run tournaments without spreadsheet chaos.

A tournament product for organisers who need digital registration, scheduling, communications, score entry, and a public match-day experience.

  • Plan formats, fixtures, and timing with less guesswork.
  • Give managers and spectators a clearer live view of the day.
  • Built for grassroots blitzes, cups, and one-day tournaments.
